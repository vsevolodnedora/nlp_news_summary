# EEX Press Release - EEX Group Monthly Volumes - May 2025
###  06/05/2025  Monthly Volume Report, EEX Press Release, Monthly Volume Report 
**Leipzig, 5 June 2025** - EEX Group reports its May monthly volumes with the following highlights:
  * The **EEX Group Global Power** markets continued to see growing trading volumes, with 1,023.5 TWh, a +8% Year-on-Year growth.
  * The volume growth was both driven by the **European Power Spot** markets, totaling 74.6 TWh (+4% YoY), as well as the **European Power Derivatives** markets, reaching a total of 680.0 TWh (+8% YoY). New record volumes were reached on the British intraday market at 4.6 TWh, as well as on the Dutch intraday market at 1.9 TWh.
  * The **EEX Japanese Power Futures** market maintained its steady growth with a traded volume of 11.1 TWh (+253% YoY), while marking the 5th anniversary of operations.
  * The **EEX Group Global Natural Gas** markets reported a 28% YoY increase in volume to 792.9 TWh, driven by the **European Natural Gas Spot** market, which reached 304.3 TWh (+26% YoY) and the **European Natural Gas Derivatives** market, which achieved 468.4 TWh (+34% YoY). The Belgian ZTP Gas Futures saw an exceptional +1677% Year-on-Year growth, with a overall volume of 17.1 TWh.
  * The **EEX Group Freight** markets totalled 85,409 contracts, marking a 31% Year-on-Year growth, driven by the EEX Freight Options growth of 90%.
  * In May, **North American Environmentals** at Nodal added up to a total of 59,176 contracts (+14% YoY).

**EEX Group** builds secure, successful and sustainable commodity markets worldwide – together with its customers. The group offers trading in power, natural gas, environmental products, freight and agriculturals as well as subsequent clearing and registry services, connecting a network of more than 950 trading participants. EEX Group consists of European Energy Exchange (EEX), EPEX SPOT, EEX Asia, Power Exchange Central Europe (PXE), GET Baltic and Nodal Exchange as well as the registry provider Grexel Systems and software companies KB Tech and Lacima. Clearing is provided by EEX Group’s clearing houses European Commodity Clearing (ECC) and Nodal Clear. EEX Group is part of Deutsche Börse Group.
